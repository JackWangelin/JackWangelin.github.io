Onsite Research: Virgin Hotel Chicago

Key Insights:

  • Implied dividers creat private socializing.
  • Be ready for when your guest is ready.
  • Clean branding can also be clean wayfinding


The Virgin Hotels exemplify much of what Moxy aims to be: young, tech-savvy... Millennial. Yet it still has aires of old world class and elegance.

Our research at the Virgin centered around its unique gathering space,
the 203 Café, located at the base of the hotel. This space wants to engender a social atmosphere, & is billed as the launch point to your plans outside the hotel, morning, afternoon, or night.
