Project Basics

Goals

  • Create a protocol for Marriott International to cater towards a younger, more international businesses traveler at Moxy.
  • This protocol must allow that traveler to maintain productivity, increase autonomy, & enjoyment, engendering a lean-service style for Marriott
  • Marriott must have a method to introduce the elements of this protocol.

Client

Moxy Hotels: a Marriott International Brand

Building upon a successful introduction in Europe, Moxy’s fun, spirited brand will launch in eight US metropolitan locations, including properties in New York City, San Francisco, Seattle, New Orleans and Chicago. Moxy’s first New York property will be created by the world-renowned design firm Yabu Pushelberg. The firm’s leaders, George Yabu and Glenn Pushelberg, are widely known for delivering the unexpected and are dedicated to maintaining designs that reflect an imaginative, thoughtful and honest approach.
