On-Screen Legibility

You're doing work for me. Right now. You've been randomly presented with one of three different stylesheets. The only variance between these style sheets are the background & text colours.

Why, you ask? Eye-strain. Aren't you tired of reading black text on a bright white lightbulb? Have you purchased "computer glasses" or blue-blocker already? Are your eyeballs drying-out, shriviling to one tenth of their natural size, from your unblinking dedication to reading on a computer screen?

I am. I have. Mine Do. I want a better reading & legibility experience on a computer screen. I think there is a better option to create that perceived contrast, I think there is an option c. Help me find it.

Please let me know if you preferred your viewing experience below.
