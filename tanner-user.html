Moxy Persona: Tanner Åshat

As a user, I want:

  • to be informed of room availability.
  • to meet new people.
  • an efficient check out method.
"I work hard, I play harder."


Who is Tanner?

Tanner is in his early-twenties and travels to new locations for short bursts of time for his sales job. Whenever he can, he likes to stay in new cities for an extra night so he can check out bars, restaurants, and clubs.

Features Needed

Tanner is a procrastinator by nature and often makes plans on the fly. He likes to go wherever he wants when he wants. A lot of his plans are contingent on whether or not his peers are also going out. Due to his spontaneous nature, efficient checkin/checkout processes are a must. Though he likes to meet new people, he’s also lazy, so if he doesn’t have to go far to do something, he won’t unless otherwise encouraged to do so.

Behaviors and Habits

Tanner is very social, though he waits until the last minute to do most things. When he’s in the mood to go out, he often texts a friend or two to see what their plans are. When he’s traveling alone, he’ll go down to the bar and chat with people there.

Goals

Tanner needs an inexpensive place to crash, though he’s willing to pay a little more if the restaurant or lounge is the kind of place where he can meet new people.


How was Tanner made?

Tanner was created from a bulk of research defining the prototypical millennial, the Silicon Valley start-up junkie, the guy who definitely mixes business with pleasure and who is definitely not James Bond.

Our primary sources were user surveys and interviews. These participants were screened for demographic eligibility, business travel experience & behaviour, including social media use & what they like to do during business travel.
