Option C.

Option c is what you want, the thing that, when you look at the offerings, you think, “why the hell can’t I just have this waffle with fried chicken and ice cream?” It is the option that fits, the option that the system abhors, but that fills the vacuum between dreams & reality.

Contact Jack to find your option c.

People.

People on the other hand, could use a good talking to; I want to be the one talking. I want to change how people think through design. By taking their brain by the wrinkles & stuffing option c into their minds, by showing the user what they want can be an option. I want to change how people think.
